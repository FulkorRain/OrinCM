I'm excited to present our comic books, Space Dracula and The Demon Gate. These are from a set of stories called the Triptych Files which are a long time in the making soon to feature a board game set in this world of weird espionage horror.

I've long been interested in the combination of spies and monsters. I remembered only recently that i'd actually written some short stories in a similair vein way back in the 90s when i was like 12 years old. I even managed to get one published in a local Alberta paper. and i was delighted to see that their website is amazingly still online! Very old-school haha. There's something uniquely charming about Web 1.0
