Hampton's Haven

All last week my class learned about the prehistoric. I already knew about dinosaurs but now I know Alberta was once underwater and called a seebed because you could see underwater animals just lying and floating everywhere. I had a bad dream that everything here became underwater again but mommy says that is millions of years ago and now it's dry and grass. I felt at home underwater in the dream but when I woke up it scared me.

My uncle has a friend that works in Waxwing Basin and she says when we go that she will show me where they found fossils of the underwater animals that look like pretty spirals that used to sleep down there. When my uncle helped me to build this website for Hampton he even made it so I can be in a web ring with them! When mommy took me to the dinosaur museum I also had dreams after that they were made alive again, but that dream didn't scare me because I like dinosaurs and I know they like me back.
